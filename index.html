About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I am Devi Srinivas, a final-year B.Tech student in Computer Science Engineering at VSM College of Engineering, Ramachandrapuram. I have a deep-seated passion for data science, with a focus on harnessing data insights to create meaningful, real-world solutions.

Throughout my academic journey, I have engaged in several data-focused projects that highlight my technical skills and analytical mindset. I am continually looking to expand my knowledge in data science, tackling new projects that contribute to innovative, impactful outcomes.
